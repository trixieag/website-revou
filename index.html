Our Packages

Candi Borobudur

The world's largest Buddhist temple is a stunning architectural and artistic masterpiece, also a testament to Indonesia's history and cultural heritage (Magelang, Central Java).

Pura Ulun Danu Bratan

The largest Hindu worship place in Bali and a cultural tourist destination with stunning views (Tabanan, Bali).

Nusa Penida

Filled with a great number of breathtaking attractions. Here, you can explore the stunning vast highlands and roam around the spectacular beaches decorating the edges of the island (Kepulauan Nusa Tenggara).

Raja Ampat

You will find a pristine paradise where Mother Nature and warm friendly people welcome you with all the exceptional wonders where words such as beautiful, enchanting, magnificent, and fascinating get its true physical meaning (West Papua Province).

Candi Prambanan

Prambanan Temple is the largest Hindu-style temple in Indonesia and also designated a World Cultural Heritage by UNESCO (Central Java).

Komodo Island

Komodo island is one of the wonders in the world because it is a natural habitat for Komodo dragons.
